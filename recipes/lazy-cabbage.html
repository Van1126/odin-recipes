Lazy Cabbage

My favourite all time, easy to make Lazy Cabbage is a great alternative to traditional Cabbage rolls.

A mixture of lean ground beef, italian rice, tomato paste and sour cabbage make this the perfect meal for the holidays.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 1/2 cup italian rice
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 jar of sour cabbage, sliced
  • 2 cans condensed tomato paste
  • 4 cups water
  • salt to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup sour cream

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir beef and sliced onion in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes; drain and discard grease.
  2. Stir in sliced sour cabbage, tomato paste, water (use the empty soup can to measure), salt, and pepper. Cover and reduce heat to medium low until cabbage is tender, stirring occasionally, let cook for about 1 hour.
  3. Add sour cream before serving.

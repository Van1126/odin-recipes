Beef Stew

Traditional Beef Stew

Beef stew is a traditional staple in many household around the world. With differet cultural variations, the beef stew is a classic and favourite recipe of mine.

Chopped up beef bits and diced potatoes come together to create the perfect comfort food.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds stewing beef
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion
  • 6 cups beef broth
  • 1/2 cup red wine
  • 1 pound potatoes
  • 4 carrots
  • 4 stalks celery
  • 3 tablespoons tomatoe paste
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 cup water

Steps

  1. Combine oil,onion and cook onion until translucent.
  2. Toss in beef and cook until browned. Add diced potatoes to pot.
  3. Add beef broth and red wine while scraping up any brown bits on the pan.
  4. Stir in all remaining ingredients add 1 cup water and reduce heat to a medium low, cover and simmer 1 hour.

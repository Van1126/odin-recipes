Oven Baked Potatoes

Crispy, rich oven baked potatoes with onion and bacon.

A delicious quick side dish full of flavour.

Ingredients

  • 5 pounds red potatoes, diced in four
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 6 slices of bacon
  • 1/2 cup butter, diced
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400F
  2. butter the baking dish
  3. In large bowl combine potatoes, bacon, and onion, add salt and pepper for taste and mix together
  4. Add the whole mixture in baking dish and cook for 1 hour, checking it periodically
